About US

OKPlate is an online app that provides online validation of Victorian number plates.

Subscriber vs Public

Subscribers can use OKPlate! to capture the image of a plate or upload an existing plate image, then automatically determine the number and validate.

Public users can manually enter a plate number into OKPlate! to validate the number.


To subscribe to OKPlate! contact our friendly staff at OKPlate!@deakin.edu.au

OKPlate! Results

One of the following validation results will be provided:

  • VALID - The entered or captured plate is a valid plate number

  • INVALID - The entered or captured plate does not exist

  • INVALID - The entered or captured plate has been recorded as Stolen - contact authorities

  • INVALID - The entered or catpture plate exists but is attached to the Wrong Car (different registration)

    Note: This feature is avaiable only when the car and plate image is uploaded or captured and the system can detect the car make
